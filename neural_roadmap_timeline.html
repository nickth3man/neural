⛔ Explicit Non-Goals

Don't implement yet: Multi-user auth, persistent chat history DB, remote embedding APIs, ANN indexes, enterprise SSO, fine-tuned models.

These violate current scope per ADR 002 or require corpus scale not yet justified.
